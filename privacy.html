Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 17, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Health Food Video Manager ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our TikTok video management service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • TikTok User ID: To authenticate and authorize video uploads to your TikTok account
  • Access Token: Temporary token provided by TikTok for API access
  • Video Files: Videos you choose to upload through the App

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Upload videos to your TikTok account with your authorization
  • Tag products in your videos using TikTok Shop API
  • Provide the core functionality of the App

4. Data Storage

We do not permanently store your personal data. All information is processed in real-time and used only for the immediate purpose of uploading content to TikTok.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With TikTok, as necessary to provide the service
  • When required by law

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke the App's access to your TikTok account at any time
  • Request deletion of any data we may have
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

7. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information during transmission and processing.

8. TikTok's Privacy Policy

Your use of TikTok is also subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review it.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through the App interface or TikTok Shop.
